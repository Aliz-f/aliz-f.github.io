About Me

I am Alireza Fadaei, a graduate of the M.Sc. program in Software Engineering at Azad University, Najafabad (2022–2025), where my thesis focused on developing an optimized federated deep learning framework for intrusion detection in dew computing environments. This work builds on my academic experience in computer networks, machine learning, and cybersecurity, and has led to a forthcoming publication based on my thesis research. Alongside my academic path, I have built a professional career in software engineering, including developing patch and vulnerability management tools at ISTT’s Fardaad, contributing to backend systems for startups, and currently teaching programming and software development at Iran TVTO. My career brings together academic inquiry and practical engineering, applying artificial intelligence and secure system design to address emerging challenges in IoT and cloud-edge infrastructures.
